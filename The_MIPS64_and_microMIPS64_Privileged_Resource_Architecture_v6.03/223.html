R/W Undefined Required 1. The TS bit originally indicated a “TLB Shutdown” condition in which circuits detected multiple TLB matches and shutdown the TLB to prevent physical damage. In newer designs, multiple TLB matches do not cause physical damage to the TLB structure, so the TS bit retains its name, but is simply an indicator to the machine check exception handler that multiple TLB matches were detected and reported by the processor.
