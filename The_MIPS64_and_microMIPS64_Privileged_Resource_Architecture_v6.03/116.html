COP2 instructions include MFC2, DMFC2, CFC2, MFHC2, MTC2, DMTC2, CTC2, MTHC2. Such an exception is used by the operating system to save and restore the state of the MDMX accumulator on a context switch (analogous to the save and restore of the FPRs).
