Description: GPR[rd]  LO The contents of special register LO are loaded into GPR rd. Restrictions: Availability and Compatibility: This instruction has been removed in Release 6. Operation: GPR[rd]  LO Exceptions: None Historical Information: In the MIPS I, II, and III architectures, the two instructions which follow the MFLO must not modify the HI register. If this restriction is violated, the result of the MFLO is UNPREDICTABLE.
