The specific syntax is implementation dependent. Purpose: Doubleword Move to Coprocessor 2 To move a doubleword from a GPR to a coprocessor 2 register. Description: CP2CPR[Impl]  GPR[rt] The contents GPR rt are loaded into the coprocessor 2 register denoted by the Impl field. The interpretation of the Impl field is left entirely to the Coprocessor 2 implementation and is not specified by the architecture. Restrictions: The results are UNPREDICTABLE if Impl specifies a coprocessor 2 register that does not exist, or if the coproces- sor 2 register specified by rd and sel is a 32-bit register.
