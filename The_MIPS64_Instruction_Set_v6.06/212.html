DSLL IDoubleword Shift Left Logical The MIPS64® Instruction Set Reference Manual, Revision 6.06 231 Format: DSLL rd, rt, sa MIPS64 Purpose: Doubleword Shift Left Logical To execute a left-shift of a doubleword by a fixed amount—0 to 31 bits Description: GPR[rd]  GPR[rt] << sa The 64-bit doubleword contents of GPR rt are shifted left, inserting zeros into the emptied bits; the result is placed in GPR rd. The bit-shift amount in the range 0 to 31 is specified by sa.
