For example, a disassembler that looks only at the primary opcode field (instruction bits 31-26) to decode BLEZL without checking that the “rt” field is zero violates the pre-Release 6 architecture specifica- tion. Complete instruction decode allows reuse of pre-Release 6 BLEZL opcode for Release 6 conditional branches.
