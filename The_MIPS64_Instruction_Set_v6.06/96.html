CTIs include all branches and jumps, NAL, ERET, ERETNC, DERET, WAIT, and PAUSE. If a control transfer instruction (CTI) is placed in the forbidden slot of a compact branch, Release 6 implementations are required to signal a Reserved Instruction exception, but only when the branch is not taken. Availability and Compatibility: These instructions are introduced by and required as of Release 6. • BEQZC reuses the opcode assigned to pre-Release 6 LDC2. • BNEZC reuses the opcode assigned to pre-Release 6 SDC2. • BEQC reuses the opcode assigned to pre-Release 6 ADDI. • BNEC reuses the opcode assigned to pre-Release 6 MIPD64 DADDI.
