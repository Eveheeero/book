DSRL IDoubleword Shift Right Logical The MIPS64® Instruction Set Reference Manual, Revision 6.06 237 Format: DSRL rd, rt, sa MIPS64 Purpose: Doubleword Shift Right Logical To execute a logical right-shift of a doubleword by a fixed amount0 to 31 bits. Description: GPR[rd]  GPR[rt] >> sa (logical) The doubleword contents of GPR rt are shifted right, inserting zeros into the emptied bits; the result is placed in GPR rd. The bit-shift amount in the range 0 to 31 is specified by sa.
