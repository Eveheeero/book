COP2 instructions include MFC2, DMFC2, CFC2, MFHC2, MTC2, DMTC2, CTC2, MTHC2. NOTE: In Release 2 of the MIPS32 Architecture, the use of COP3 as a user-defined coprocessor has been removed. The use of COP3 is reserved for the future extension of the architecture.
