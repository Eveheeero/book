SRL IShift Word Right Logical MIPS® Architecture for Programmers Volume II-B: microMIPS64™ Instruction Set, Revision 6.05 407 Format: SRL rt, rs, sa microMIPS Purpose: Shift Word Right Logical To execute a logical right-shift of a word by a fixed number of bits. Description: GPR[rt]  GPR[rs] >> sa (logical) The contents of the low-order 32-bit word of GPR rs are shifted right, inserting zeros into the emptied bits. The word result is sign-extended and placed in GPR rt. The bit-shift amount is specified by sa. Restrictions: On 64-bit processors, if GPR rs does not contain a sign-extended 32-bit value (bits 63..31 equal), then the result of the operation is UNPREDICTABLE.
