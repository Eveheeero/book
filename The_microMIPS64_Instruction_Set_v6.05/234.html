Description: GPR[rt]  SwapHalfwordsWithinDoublewords(GPR[rs]) The halfwords of GPR rs are swapped and stored in GPR rt. Restrictions: In implementations of Release 1 of the architecture, this instruction resulted in a Reserved Instruction exception.
