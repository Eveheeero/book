DSLLV IDoubleword Shift Left Logical Variable MIPS® Architecture for Programmers Volume II-B: microMIPS64™ Instruction Set, Revision 6.05 237 Format: DSLLV rd, rt, rs microMIPS64 Purpose: Doubleword Shift Left Logical Variable To execute a left-shift of a doubleword by a variable number of bits. Description: GPR[rd]  GPR[rt] << GPR[rs] The 64-bit doubleword contents of GPR rt are shifted left, inserting zeros into the emptied bits; the result is placed in GPR rd. The bit-shift amount in the range 0 to 63 is specified by the low-order 6 bits in GPR rs.
