DSRA Doubleword Shift Right Arithmetic 238 MIPS® Architecture for Programmers Volume II-B: microMIPS64™ Instruction Set, Revision 6.05 Format: DSRA rt, rs, sa microMIPS64 Purpose: Doubleword Shift Right Arithmetic To execute an arithmetic right-shift of a doubleword by a fixed amount—0 to 31 bits. Description: GPR[rt]  GPR[rs] >> sa (arithmetic) The 64-bit doubleword contents of GPR rs are shifted right, duplicating the sign bit (63) into the emptied bits; the result is placed in GPR rt. The bit-shift amount in the range 0 to 31 is specified by sa.
