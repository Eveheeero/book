BITSWAP is a 32-bit instruction. BITSWAP operates on all 4 bytes of a 32-bit GPR on a 32-bit CPU. On a 64-bit CPU, BITSWAP operates on the low 4 bytes, sign extending to 64-bits. DBITSWAP operates on all 8 bytes of a 64-bit GPR on a 64-bit CPU. Restrictions: BITSWAP: None. Availability and Compatibility: The BITSWAP instruction is introduced by and required as of Release 6.
