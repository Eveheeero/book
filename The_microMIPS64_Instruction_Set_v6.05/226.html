The specific syntax is implementation dependent. Purpose: Doubleword Move from Coprocessor 2 To move a doubleword from a coprocessor 2 register to a GPR. Description: GPR[rt]  CP2CPR[Impl] The contents of the coprocessor 2 register denoted by the Impl field is loaded into GPR rt. The interpretation of the Impl field is left entirely to the Coprocessor 2 implementation and is not specified by the architecture. Restrictions: The results are UNPREDICTABLE if Impl specifies a coprocessor 2 register that does not exist, or if the coproces- sor 2 register specified by rd and sel is a 32-bit register.
