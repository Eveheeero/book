SRL IShift Word Right Logical The MIPS32® Instruction Set Manual, Revision 6.06 385 Format: SRL rd, rt, sa MIPS32 Purpose: Shift Word Right Logical To execute a logical right-shift of a word by a fixed number of bits. Description: GPR[rd]  GPR[rt] >> sa (logical) The contents of the low-order 32-bit word of GPR rt are shifted right, inserting zeros into the emptied bits. The word result is placed in GPR rd. The bit-shift amount is specified by sa.
