NOR Not Or 313 The MIPS32® Instruction Set Manual, Revision 6.06 Format: NOR rd, rs, rt MIPS32 Purpose: Not Or To do a bitwise logical NOT OR. Description: GPR[rd]  GPR[rs] nor GPR[rt] The contents of GPR rs are combined with the contents of GPR rt in a bitwise logical NOR operation. The result is placed into GPR rd.
