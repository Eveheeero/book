If the alignment check exception (#AC) is enabled (and the CPL is 3), signaling of #AC is not guaranteed and may vary with implementation, as follows. In all implementations where #AC is not signaled, a general protection exception is signaled in its place. In addition, the width of the alignment check may also vary with implementation. For instance, for a given implementation, an align- ment check exception might be signaled for a 2-byte misalignment, whereas a general protec- tion exception might be signaled for all other misalignments (4-, 8-, or 16-byte misalignments).
