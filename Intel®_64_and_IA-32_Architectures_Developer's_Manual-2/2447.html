Vol. 2D B-69 INSTRUCTION FORMATS AND ENCODINGS B.15 SSE4.2 FORMATS AND ENCODING TABLE The tables in this section provide SSE4.2 formats and encodings. Some SSE4.2 instructions require a mandatory prefix (66H, F2H, F3H) as part of the three-byte opcode. These prefixes are included in the tables. In 64-bit mode, some instructions requires REX.W, the byte sequence of REX.W prefix in the opcode sequence is shown.
