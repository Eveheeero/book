706 VPBROADCASTW Instruction Reference AMD64 Technology 26568—Rev. 3.25—November 2021 Loads a word from a register or memory and writes it to all 8 or 16 words of an XMM or YMM reg- ister. This instruction has both 128-bit and 256-bit encodings: XMM Encoding Copies the source operand to all 8 words of the destination. The source operand is the least-significant 16 bits of an XMM register or a 16-bit memory location. The destination is an XMM register. Bits [255:128] of the YMM register that corresponds to the des- tination are cleared. YMM Encoding Copies the source operand to all 16 words of the destination. The source operand is the least-significant 16 bits of an XMM register or a 16-bit memory location. The destination is a YMM register.
