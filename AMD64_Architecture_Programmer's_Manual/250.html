For CMPSS the first source operand is also the destination. The (V)CMPSD instruction performs an analogous operation on double-precision floating-point values. CMPSD leaves the high-order quadword of the destination XMM register unmodified. VCMPSD copies the high-order quadword of the first source operand to the destination.
