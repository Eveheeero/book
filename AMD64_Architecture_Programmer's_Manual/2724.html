Instruction Reference VPHADDBQ 763 26568—Rev. 3.25—November 2021 AMD64 Technology Adds two sets of eight 8-bit signed integer values of the source and packs the sign-extended sums into the corresponding quadword of the destination. There are two operands: VPHADDBQ dest, src The destination is an XMM register and the source is either an XMM register or a 128-bit memory location. Bits [255:128] of the corresponding YMM register are cleared.
