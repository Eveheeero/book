Instruction Reference PMADDUBSW, VPMADDUBSW 363 26568—Rev. 3.25—November 2021 AMD64 Technology Multiplies and adds sets of two packed 8-bit unsigned values from the first source operand and two packed 8-bit signed values from the second source operand, with signed saturation; writes eight 16-bit sums to the destination. For the 128-bit form of the instruction, the following operations are performed: dest is the destination register – either an XMM register or the corresponding YMM register. src1 is the first source operand. src2 is the second source operand.
