356 General-Purpose Instruction Reference AMD64 Technology 24594—Rev. 3.35—June 2023 These opcodes generate an invalid opcode exception. Unlike other undefined opcodes that may be defined as legal instructions in the future, these opcodes are intended to stay undefined. On some AMD64 processor implementations, UD1 may report an invalid opcode exception regardless of whether fetching the ModRM byte could trigger a paging or segmentation exception.
