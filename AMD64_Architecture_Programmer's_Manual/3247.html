x87 Floating-Point FFREE 249 Instruction Reference 26569—Rev. 3.16—November 2021 AMD64 Technology Frees the specified x87 stack register by marking its tag register entry as empty. The instruction does not affect the contents of the freed register or the top-of-stack pointer (TOP).
