For the sake of identifying a specific element within a vector (packed) data type, the elements are numbered from right to left starting with 0 and ending with (vector_size/element_size) −1. Some instructions operate on even and odd pairs of elements. The even elements are (0, 2, 4 ...) and the odd elements are (1,3,5 ...). Software can interpret data in ways other than those listed —such fixed-point or fractional numbers— but the SSE instructions do not directly support such interpretations and software must handle them entirely on its own. 128-bit Vector Data Types Figure 4-6 below illustrates the 128-bit vector data types.
