986 AMD64 Technology 26568—Rev. 3.25—November 2021 A.7 Encryption and Decryption The AMD64 architecture implements the AES algorithm by means of an iterative function called a round for both encryption and the inverse operation, decryption. The top-level encryption and decryption procedures Encrypt( ) and Decrypt( ) set up the rounds and invoke the functions that perform them.
