System Resources 52 24593—Rev. 3.41—June 2023 AMD64 Technology • Status bits provide information resulting from logical and arithmetic operations. These are written by the processor and can be read by software running at any privilege level. Figure 3-8 on page 52 shows the format of the RFLAGS register. The legacy EFLAGS register is identical to the low 32 bits of the register shown in Figure 3-8 (RFLAGS bits 31:0). The term rFLAGS is used to refer to the 16-bit, 32-bit, or 64-bit flags register, depending on context. Figure 3-8.
