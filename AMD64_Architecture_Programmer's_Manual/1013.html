SLB Example Layout 15.27.4 Trusted Platform Module The trusted platform module, or TPM, is an essential part of full trusted system initialization. This device is attached to an LPC link off the system I/O hub. It recognizes special SKINIT transactions, receives the SL image sent by SKINIT and verifies the signature. Based on the outcome, the device decides whether or not to cooperate with the SL or subsequent SK. The TPM typically contains sealed storage containing cryptographic keys and other high-security information that may be specific to the platform.
