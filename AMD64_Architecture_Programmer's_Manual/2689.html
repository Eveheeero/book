728 VPERM2I128 Instruction Reference AMD64 Technology 26568—Rev. 3.25—November 2021 Copies 128 bits of integer data from a selected octword of two 256-bit source operands or zero to each octword of a 256-bit destination, as specified by an immediate byte operand. The immediate operand is encoded as follows. This is a 256-bit extended-form instruction: The first source operand is a YMM register and the second source operand is either a YMM register or a 256-bit memory location. The destination is a third YMM register. Bits 2 and 6 of the immediate byte are ignored.
