Instruction Reference VPHADDUBQ 771 26568—Rev. 3.25—November 2021 AMD64 Technology Adds two sets of eight 8-bit unsigned integer values from the second source and packs the sums into the corresponding quadword of the destination. There are two operands: VPHADDUBQ dest, src The destination is an XMM register and the source is either an XMM register or a 128-bit memory location. When the destination XMM register is written, bits [255:128] of the corresponding YMM register are cleared.
