A.1.5 Extended Instruction Opcode Maps The following sections present the VEX and the XOP extended instruction opcode maps. The VEX.map_select field of the three-byte VEX encoding escape sequence selects VEX opcode maps: 01h, 02h, or 03h. The two-byte VEX encoding escape sequence implicitly selects the VEX map 01h. The XOP.map_select field selects between the three XOP maps: 08h, 09h or 0Ah. VEX Opcode Maps.
