Instruction Reference VPHSUBDQ 787 26568—Rev. 3.25—November 2021 AMD64 Technology Subtracts the most significant signed integer doubleword from the least significant signed integer doubleword of each quadword in the source and packs the sign-extended 64-bit integer differences into the corresponding quadword element of the destination. There are two operands: VPHSUBDQ dest, src The destination is an XMM register and the source is either an XMM register or a 128-bit memory location. When the destination is written, bits [255:128] of the corresponding YMM register are cleared.
