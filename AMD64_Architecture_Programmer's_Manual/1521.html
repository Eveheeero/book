254 General-Purpose Instruction Reference AMD64 Technology 24594—Rev. 3.35—June 2023 Multiplies the unsigned byte, word, doubleword, or quadword value in the specified register or memory location by the value in AL, AX, EAX, or RAX and stores the result in AX, DX:AX, EDX:EAX, or RDX:RAX (depending on the operand size). It puts the high-order bits of the product in AH, DX, EDX, or RDX. If the upper half of the product is non-zero, the instruction sets the carry flag (CF) and overflow flag (OF) both to 1. Otherwise, it clears CF and OF to 0. The other arithmetic flags (SF, ZF, AF, PF) are undefined.
