As the DEV_OP and DEV_DATA registers are accessed through PCI config space (ports 0CF8h–0CFFh), they may be secured from unauthorized access by software executing on the processor by appropriate settings in the SVM I/O protection bitmap. These registers are also protected by the host bridge from external access as described in “Config Space Accesses” on page 541. 15.24.6 DEV Control and Status Registers The DEV control and status registers are accessible by means of the indirection mechanism; these registers are not directly visible in PCI config space.
