This overview uses the data type GF256 as an alias of nat, to identify variables that are to be thought of as elements of GF. The operations of addition and multiplication in GF are denoted by ⊕ and , respectively. Since 2 is of characteristic 2, addition is simply the “exclusive or” operation: x ⊕ y = x^ y In particular, every element of GF is its own additive inverse.
