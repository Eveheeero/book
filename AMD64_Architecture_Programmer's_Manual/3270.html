272 FLDLG2 x87 Floating-Point Instruction Reference AMD64 Technology 26569—Rev. 3.16—November 2021 Pushes log 10 2 onto the x87 register stack. The value in ST(0) is the result, in double-extended- precision format, of rounding an internal 66-bit constant according to the setting of the RC field in the x87 control word register.
