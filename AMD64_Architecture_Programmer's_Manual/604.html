149 Page Translation and Protection AMD64 Technology 24593—Rev. 3.41—June 2023 Figure 5-29. 2-Mbyte PDE—Long Mode 5.3.5 1-Gbyte Page Translation In long mode, 1-Gbyte physical-page translation is performed by dividing the virtual address into up to five fields. Up to three of the fields are used as indices into the level page-translation hierarchy. The virtual-address fields are described as follows, and are shown in Figure 5-30 on page 150 (1-Gbyte PDPE—Long Mode) and Figure 5-31 on page 151 (1-Gbyte Page Translation—Long Mode 5-Level Paging): • Bits 63:57 are a sign extension of bit 56 as required for canonical address forms. • Bits 56:48 index into the 512-entry page-map level-5 table.
