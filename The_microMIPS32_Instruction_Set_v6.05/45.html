E.g. sign_extend(immediate16) or sign_extend(disp9). However, sometimes it is necessary to specify the bit position. For example, sign_extend(temp 31..0 ) or the more complicated (offset 15 ) GPRLEN-(16+2) || offset || 0 2 . The explicit notation sign_extend.nbits(val) or sign_extend(val,nbits) is suggested as a simpli- fication. They say to sign extend as if an nbits-sized signed integer. The width to be sign extended to is usually appar- ent by context, and is usually GPRLEN, 32 or 64 bits.
