SLLV IShift Word Left Logical Variable MIPS® Architecture for Programmers Volume II-B: microMIPS32™ Instruction Set, Revision 6.05 326 Format: SLLV rd, rt, rs microMIPS Purpose: Shift Word Left Logical Variable To left-shift a word by a variable number of bits. Description: GPR[rd]  GPR[rt] << GPR[rs] The contents of the low-order 32-bit word of GPR rt are shifted left, inserting zeros into the emptied bits. The result- ing word is placed in GPR rd. The bit-shift amount is specified by the low-order 5 bits of GPR rs.
