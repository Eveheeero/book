The specific syntax is implementation-dependent. Purpose: Move Word to Coprocessor 2 To copy a word from a GPR to a COP2 general register. Description: CP2CPR[Impl]  GPR[rt] The low word in GPR rt is placed into the low word of a Coprocessor 2 general register denoted by the Impl field. The interpretation of the Impl field is left entirely to the Coprocessor 2 implementation and is not specified by the architecture. Restrictions: The results are UNPREDICTABLE if the Impl field specifies a Coprocessor 2 register that does not exist.
