Bit 08: AVX512_VP2INTERSECT. Bit 09: SRBDS_CTRL. If 1, enumerates support for the IA32_MCU_OPT_CTRL MSR and indicates its bit 0 (RNGDS_MITG_DIS) is also supported. Bit 10: MD_CLEAR supported.
