6-24 Vol. 1 PROCEDURE CALLS, INTERRUPTS, AND EXCEPTIONS 6.6.2 LEAVE Instruction The LEAVE instruction, which does not have any operands, reverses the action of the previous ENTER instruction. The LEAVE instruction copies the contents of the EBP register into the ESP register to release all stack space allo- cated to the procedure. Then it restores the old value of the EBP register from the stack. This simultaneously restores the ESP register to its original value.
