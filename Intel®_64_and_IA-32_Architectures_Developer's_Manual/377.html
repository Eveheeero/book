Vol. 1 14-31 PROGRAMMING WITH INTEL® AVX, FMA, AND INTEL® AVX2 14.7.1 Detection of Intel® AVX2 Hardware support for AVX2 is indicated by CPUID.(EAX=07H, ECX=0H):EBX.AVX2[bit 5]=1. Application Software must identify that hardware supports AVX, after that it must also detect support for AVX2 by checking CPUID.(EAX=07H, ECX=0H):EBX.AVX2[bit 5].
