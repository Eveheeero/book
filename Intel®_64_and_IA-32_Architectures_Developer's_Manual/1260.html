See the summary chart at the beginning of this section for encoding data and limits. If CR4.PCIDE = 1, bit 63 of the source operand to MOV to CR3 determines whether the instruction invalidates entries in the TLBs and the paging-structure caches (see Section 4.10.4.1, “Operations that Invalidate TLBs and Paging-Structure Caches,” in the Intel ® 64 and IA-32 Architectures Software Developer’s Manual, Volume 3A). The instruction does not modify bit 63 of CR3, which is reserved and always 0. See “Changes to Instruction Behavior in VMX Non-Root Operation” in Chapter 26 of the Intel ® 64 and IA-32 Archi- tectures Software Developer’s Manual, Volume 3C, for more information about the behavior of this instruction in VMX non-root operation.
