31-26 Vol. 3C VMX INSTRUCTION REFERENCE VMXOFF—Leave VMX Operation Instruction Operand Encoding Description Takes the logical processor out of VMX operation, unblocks INIT signals, conditionally re-enables A20M, and clears any address-range monitoring. 1 Operation IF (not in VMX operation) or (CR0.PE = 0) or (RFLAGS.VM = 1) or (IA32_EFER.LMA = 1 and CS.L = 0) THEN #UD; ELSIF in VMX non-root operation THEN VMexit; ELSIF CPL > 0 THEN #GP(0); ELSIF dual-monitor treatment of SMIs and SMM is active THEN VMfail(VMXOFF under dual-monitor treatment of SMIs and SMM); ELSE leave VMX operation; unblock INIT; IF IA32_SMM_MONITOR_CTL[2] = 0 2 THEN unblock SMIs; IF outside SMX operation 3 THEN unblock and enable A20M; FI; clear address-range monitoring; VMsucceed; FI; Flags Affected See the operation section and Section 31.2.
