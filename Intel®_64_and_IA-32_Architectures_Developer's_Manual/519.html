A review of this notation makes the manual easier to read. 1.3.1 Bit and Byte Order In illustrations of data structures in memory, smaller addresses appear toward the bottom of the figure; addresses increase toward the top. Bit positions are numbered from right to left. The numerical value of a set bit is equal to two raised to the power of the bit position.
