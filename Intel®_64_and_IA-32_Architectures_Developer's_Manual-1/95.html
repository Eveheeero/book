Vol. 1 4-9 DATA TYPES 4.7 BCD AND PACKED BCD INTEGERS Binary-coded decimal integers (BCD integers) are unsigned 4-bit integers with valid values ranging from 0 to 9. IA- 32 architecture defines operations on BCD integers located in one or more general-purpose registers or in one or more x87 FPU registers (see Figure 4-9).
