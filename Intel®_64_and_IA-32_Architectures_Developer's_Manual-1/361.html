Vol. 1 14-17 PROGRAMMING WITH INTEL速 AVX, FMA, AND INTEL速 AVX2 14.3.1 Detection of VEX-Encoded AES and VPCLMULQDQ The VAESDEC/VAESDECLAST/VAESENC/VAESENCLAST/VAESIMC/VAESKEYGENASSIST instructions operate on YMM states. The detection sequence must combine checking for CPUID.1:ECX.AES[bit 25] = 1 and the sequence for detection application support for Intel AVX. Similarly, the detection sequence for VPCLMULQDQ must combine checking for CPUID.1:ECX.PCLMULQDQ[bit 1] = 1 and the sequence for detection application support for Intel AVX. This is shown in the pseudocode provided in Example 14-3. Example 14-2.
