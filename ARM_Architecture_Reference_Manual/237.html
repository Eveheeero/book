A4-87 Usage To construct the word in Rd consisting of the top half of register Ra and the bottom half of register Rb as its most and least significant halfwords respectively, use: PKHBT Rd, Rb, Ra To construct the word in Rd consisting of the bottom half of register Ra and the bottom half of register Rb as its most and least significant halfwords respectively, use: PKHBT Rd, Rb, Ra, LSL #16 Notes Use of R15 Specifying R15 for register <Rd>, <Rm>, or <Rn> has UNPREDICTABLE results.
