A4-185 Usage You can use SSUBADDX for operations on complex numbers that are held as pairs of 16-bit integers or Q15 numbers. If you hold the real and imaginary parts of a complex number in the bottom and top half of a register respectively, then the instruction: SSUBADDX Rd, Ra, Rb performs the complex arithmetic operation Rd = Ra - i * Rb. Notes Use of R15 Specifying R15 for register <Rd>, <Rm>, or <Rn> has UNPREDICTABLE results.
