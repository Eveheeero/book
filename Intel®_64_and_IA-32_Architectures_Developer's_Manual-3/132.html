4-26 Vol. 3A PAGING Figure 4-11 gives a summary of the formats of CR3 and the 4-level and 5-level paging-structure entries. For the paging structure entries, it identifies separately the format of entries that map pages, those that reference other paging structures, and those that do neither because they are “not present”; bit 0 (P) and bit 7 (PS) are highlighted because they determine how a paging-structure entry is used.
