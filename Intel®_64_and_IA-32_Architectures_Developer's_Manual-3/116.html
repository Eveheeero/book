4-10 Vol. 3A PAGING With 32-bit paging, there are reserved bits only if CR4.PSE = 1: • If the P flag and the PS flag (bit 7) of a PDE are both 1, the bits reserved depend on MAXPHYADDR, and whether the PSE-36 mechanism is supported: 1 — If the PSE-36 mechanism is not supported, bits 21:13 are reserved. — If the PSE-36 mechanism is supported, bits 21:(M–19) are reserved, where M is the minimum of 40 and MAXPHYADDR. • If the PAT is not supported: 2 — If the P flag of a PTE is 1, bit 7 is reserved. — If the P flag and the PS flag of a PDE are both 1, bit 12 is reserved. (If CR4.PSE = 0, no bits are reserved with 32-bit paging.)
