SRA IShift Word Right Arithmetic MIPS® Architecture for Programmers Volume II-B: microMIPS32™ Instruction Set, Revision 6.05 332 Format: SRA rt, rs, sa microMIPS Purpose: Shift Word Right Arithmetic To execute an arithmetic right-shift of a word by a fixed number of bits. Description: GPR[rt]  GPR[rs] >> sa (arithmetic) The contents of the low-order 32-bit word of GPR rs are shifted right, duplicating the sign-bit (bit 31) in the emptied bits; the word result is placed in GPR rt. The bit-shift amount is specified by sa.
