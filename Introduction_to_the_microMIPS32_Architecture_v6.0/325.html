SLL Shift Word Left Logical 325 MIPS® Architecture for Programmers Volume II-B: microMIPS32™ Instruction Set, Revision 6.05 Format: SLL rt, rs, sa microMIPS Purpose: Shift Word Left Logical To left-shift a word by a fixed number of bits. Description: GPR[rt]  GPR[rs] << sa The contents of the low-order 32-bit word of GPR rs are shifted left, inserting zeros into the emptied bits. The word result is placed in GPR rt. The bit-shift amount is specified by sa.
