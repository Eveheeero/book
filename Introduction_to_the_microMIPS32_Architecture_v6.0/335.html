SRLV Shift Word Right Logical Variable 335 MIPS® Architecture for Programmers Volume II-B: microMIPS32™ Instruction Set, Revision 6.05 Format: SRLV rd, rt, rs microMIPS Purpose: Shift Word Right Logical Variable To execute a logical right-shift of a word by a variable number of bits. Description: GPR[rd]  GPR[rt] >> GPR[rs] (logical) The contents of the low-order 32-bit word of GPR rt are shifted right, inserting zeros into the emptied bits; the word result is placed in GPR rd. The bit-shift amount is specified by the low-order 5 bits of GPR rs.
