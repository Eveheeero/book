Release 6 removes the add instructions with a large immediate field that trap on signed overflow (ADDI, DADDI), but retains the register-register forms (ADD, DADD). Release 6 includes additional instructions with large constants, as described in Section 5.4 “Address Computation and Large Constant Instructions (Release 6)”.
