It also defines an instruction to align a misaligned pair of paired-single val- ues (ALNV.PS) and a quartet of instructions that merge a pair of paired-single values (PLL.PS, PLU.PS, PUL.PS, PUU.PS). All these instructions are removed in Release 6. Table 7.19 lists the conditional move instructions.
