CPU Programming Model 41 MIPS® Architecture For Programmers Volume I-A: Introduction to the MIPS64® Architecture, Revision 6.01 • During a multiply-add or multiply-subtract operation, the HI and LO registers store the result of the integer multiply-add or multiply-subtract. • During a division, the HI and LO registers store the quotient (in LO) and remainder (in HI) of integer divide. • During a multiply-accumulate, the HI and LO registers store the accumulated result of the operation. As of Release 6, the HI and LO registers and related instructions are removed from the base instruction set architec- ture and only exist in the DSP Module.
