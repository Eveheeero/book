SLLI is a logical left shift (zeros are shifted into the lower bits); SRLI is a logical right shift (zeros are shifted into the upper bits); and SRAI is an arithmetic right shift (the original sign bit is copied into the vacated upper bits). SLLIW, SRLIW, and SRAIW are RV64I-only instructions that are analogously deﬁned but operate on 32-bit values and produce signed 32-bit results. SLLIW, SRLIW, and SRAIW encodings with imm[5] ̸= 0 are reserved.
