Perhaps the most important single event was the formation of the non-proﬁt RISC-V Foundation in August 2015. The Foundation will now take over stewardship of the oﬃcial RISC-V ISA standard, and the oﬃcial website riscv.org is the best place to obtain news and updates on the RISC-V standard.
