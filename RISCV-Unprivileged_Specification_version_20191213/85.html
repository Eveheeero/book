Implementations can always trap to machine-mode software handlers to provide exceptional default values. 11.4 Subnormal Arithmetic Operations on subnormal numbers are handled in accordance with the IEEE 754-2008 standard. In the parlance of the IEEE standard, tininess is detected after rounding. Detecting tininess after rounding results in fewer spurious underﬂow signals. 11.5 Single-Precision Load and Store Instructions Floating-point loads and stores use the same base+oﬀset addressing mode as the integer base ISA, with a base address in register rs1 and a 12-bit signed byte oﬀset.
